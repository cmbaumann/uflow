UFlow is a tool to help students organize their future classes. It is based off of the flowchart that students with CS and MIS majors recieve from their advisors.

Project Goals

  • Be able to customize a personalized flowchart for your own use, whether that be through what classes you intend to take each semester or stylizing your chart with colors.
  • Track what classes have been taken and how many hours each class will give you and indicate whether you reached the required amount of hours for your major.
  • Automatically fill in the flowchart based on what major you choose.
  • Download the customized chart as a picture through a PNG or JPG format to later print or share through email.
  • Save multiple flowcharts.
